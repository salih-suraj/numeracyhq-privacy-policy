1. Introduction
Welcome to NumeracyHQ (“we”, “our”, “us”), an educational app designed for children aged 3–8 to help them learn foundational math skills in a fun and safe environment.
We are committed to protecting the privacy of children and ensuring a secure, ad-free experience.

2. Information We Collect
NumeracyHQ is designed to minimize data collection.
We do NOT collect any personal data from users.
The app:
Does not require account creation
Does not collect names, emails, or contact details
Does not collect location data

Learning progress

Never leaves the device
Is not shared with us or any third party
